🛠️ Visual Strategy Builder

Construct trading strategies with drag‑and‑drop blocks, indicators and logic without writing code.

  • Indicator library with 50+ technical indicators
  • Conditional logic builder with AND/OR operators
  • Real‑time syntax checking and error highlighting
  • Template gallery and export/import support

🤖 Auto Strategy Generator

Harness AI and machine learning to automatically propose and evolve trading strategies.

  • Machine learning pattern recognition and genetic algorithms
  • Market regime classification and parameter exploration
  • Generation wizard with guided settings and progress tracking
  • Variant comparison and bulk selection tools

🧪 Backtesting & Validation

Evaluate strategies on historical data with realistic market conditions and advanced robustness tests.

  • High‑performance backtesting with transaction cost modelling
  • Multi‑asset support and point‑in‑time data integrity
  • Walk‑forward, Monte Carlo and statistical validation tools
  • Trade‑by‑trade analysis and report export

⚙️ Optimization & Analytics

Tune strategy parameters and analyse performance across multiple metrics and benchmarks.

  • Genetic, Bayesian and grid search optimization engines
  • Real‑time optimization progress and result visualisations
  • Performance dashboards with risk/return metrics and drawdown charts
  • Sensitivity analysis and historical optimisation analysis

📈 Ranking & Risk Management

Identify the best strategies and control portfolio risk with intelligent scoring and real‑time safeguards.

  • Customisable ranking criteria and strategy leaderboards
  • Risk metrics including VaR, CVaR and drawdown limits
  • Adaptive position sizing and automated protection mechanisms
  • Market condition heatmaps and clustering visualisations

📰 Data & News Engine

Stay informed with integrated real‑time market data feeds and sentiment‑aware news streams.

  • Real‑time quotes and market data across multiple asset classes
  • Live news feed with NLP‑driven sentiment scoring
  • Event detection, classification and relevance filtering
  • Sentiment‑based alerts and impact analysis charts

🚀 Execution & Simulation

Execute strategies live or in paper trading mode with confidence and control.

  • Multi‑broker order routing and real‑time execution monitoring
  • Paper trading environment mirroring live conditions
  • Order management with audit trails and trade history
  • Manual override controls and emergency stop actions

👥 Collaboration & API

Connect with your team and extend the platform through powerful APIs and integrations.

  • User management with roles, teams and strategy sharing
  • RESTful and WebSocket APIs with developer portal
  • Integration templates, webhooks and third‑party marketplace
  • SSO, API key management and audit logging

🤖 AI, Portfolio & Compliance

Leverage machine learning, optimise portfolios and meet regulatory standards.

  • ML‑driven strategy enhancement and predictive analytics
  • Portfolio construction, risk budgeting and rebalancing tools
  • Comprehensive audit trails and automated compliance reporting
  • Regulatory dashboards and document management
